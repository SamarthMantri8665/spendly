Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects personal financial information that you voluntarily enter into the application. This includes expense amounts, categories, dates, descriptions, and any other information you provide when logging transactions. We do not collect or store your bank account credentials or credit card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data you provide is used solely to provide you with expense tracking functionality. We use your information to generate spending summaries, category breakdowns, and other personal finance insights. Your data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. You retain ownership of all data you enter and can export or delete your data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal financial data with third parties. We may use trusted service providers for hosting and technical infrastructure, but these partners are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can export your expense history or permanently delete your account through your account settings. If you have any requests regarding your data, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the application or by email. Your continued use of Spendly after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.
